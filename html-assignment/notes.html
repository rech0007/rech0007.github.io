HTML Tags

p - Paragraphs
Adds a paragraph that has a space before and after the contents
q - Quotes
For small quotations within other content
em - Emphasis
Puts text into italics that is slightly important
strong - Important Text
Hightly important text that will be bold print
mark - Highlighting Text
Highlights a specific piece of text for reference
